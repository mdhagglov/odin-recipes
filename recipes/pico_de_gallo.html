Pico de Gallo

What you'll need:

  • 2 large tomatoes, preferably heirloom, chopped
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 2 jalapeños, finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, finely grated
  • 1/3 cup cilantro leaves with tender stems, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • Kosher salt

Preparation:

Gently toss tomatoes, onions, jalapeños, garlic, cilantro, and lime juice in a small bowl; season with salt. Let sit, uncovered, to let flavours meld, about 10 minutes.
